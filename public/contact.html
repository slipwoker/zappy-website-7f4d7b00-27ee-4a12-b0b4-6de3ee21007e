Common Questions for Your Costa Rica Adventure

Practical answers to help solo explorers, couples, and families plan their perfect itinerary with confidence.

Which airport is better — SJO or Liberia?

It depends on your first destination. Fly into SJO for the Caribbean coast, Central Valley, or southern Pacific. Choose Liberia for quick access to Guanacaste beaches, Rincon de la Vieja, and the Nicoya Peninsula.

Can the Caribbean fit into a shorter trip?

Absolutely. A 5-day itinerary from SJO can include Tortuguero or Cahuita with careful pacing. For a deeper Caribbean experience, our 7 and 10-day plans weave the coast in seamlessly.

Which itinerary length is best for families?

We recommend 7 or 10 days for families. This allows a relaxed pace with built-in downtime, kid-friendly wildlife encounters, and a mix of beach and rainforest without feeling rushed.

Is a Costa Rica adventure safe for solo travelers?

Yes — Costa Rica is one of the safest countries in Latin America for solo explorers. Our itineraries prioritize well-connected routes, reputable accommodations, and clear transport logistics so you can focus on the journey.

What makes a couples adventure different?

Couples itineraries balance adventure with romance — think private hot springs, sunset sails, boutique eco-lodges, and volcano-view dinners. We design days that feel intimate without sacrificing exploration.

Do I need a 4x4 vehicle for these trips?

Many of our recommended routes include gravel roads and rural areas where a 4x4 gives you freedom and comfort. We note vehicle recommendations clearly on each itinerary so there are no surprises.
