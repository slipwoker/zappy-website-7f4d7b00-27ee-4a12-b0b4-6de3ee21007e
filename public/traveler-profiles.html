Your Costa Rica, Your Way

The same country can feel radically different depending on who you're traveling with. Whether you're chasing solo serenity, romantic sunsets, or family-sized thrills, we've shaped three distinct lenses to help you plan the perfect 5, 7, or 10-day adventure — from both San José and Liberia airports, all the way to the Caribbean coast.

Solo Explorers

For the independent spirit craving off-the-grid trails, spontaneous detours, and pure freedom.

Couples

Romance meets adventure with private hideaways, sunset sails, and two-person itineraries.

Families

Designed for all ages, balancing wildlife encounters, safe thrills, and logistics that actually work.

Solo

Freedom to Roam

Costa Rica is a solo traveler's dream — safe, welcoming, and full of spontaneous encounters. From the volcanic north to the Caribbean coast, every route is designed for independence and ease.

  • Social hostels & eco-lodges — meet fellow adventurers in vibrant communal spaces.
  • Simple public transport & shuttles — easy connections between SJO, Liberia, and hidden coastal gems.
  • Flex your own itinerary — add a spontaneous night in Tortuguero or extend your surf stay in Santa Teresa.
Couples

Adventure for Two

Balance adrenaline with intimacy. Our couples' itineraries weave thrilling zip-line mornings with sunset hot springs and private beach dinners, creating a rhythm that feels both exciting and deeply romantic.

  • Scenic boutique stays — handpicked rooms with volcano views and private plunge pools.
  • Active days, serene evenings — hike to La Fortuna waterfall by day, soak in thermal rivers by night.
  • Romantic settings — candlelit dinners on the sand and sunrise coffee tours in the Central Valley.
Families

Wild Wonders, Together

Costa Rica is a natural playground for all ages. Our family-focused plans keep travel distances manageable, pack in wildlife-rich stops, and include activities that thrill kids and adults alike — from sloth sanctuaries to gentle river floats.

  • Short, scenic drives — routes under 3 hours with plenty of fruit stand and wildlife spotting breaks.
  • Hands-on wildlife encounters — visit rescue centers, spot howler monkeys, and watch sea turtles nest.
  • Activities for every age — gentle hanging bridges, chocolate-making workshops, and calm Caribbean beaches.

Adventures for Every Traveler

From solo escapes to family expeditions, discover how Costa Rica's diverse landscapes — including the vibrant Caribbean coast — come alive across these curated highlights.

Solo

Caribbean Village Immersion

Wander through Cahuita and Puerto Viejo, where reggae rhythms meet Afro-Caribbean cuisine. Perfect for solo explorers craving authentic cultural connections and jungle-meets-beach solitude.

Solo

Monteverde Cloud Forest Solo Trek

Zip through misty canopies and hike hanging bridges at your own pace. Monteverde's biodiversity offers the ultimate Costa Rica adventure itinerary for solo travelers seeking self-discovery.

Couples

Volcano-to-Coast Scenic Loop

Start with Arenal's thermal hot springs, then wind down to Guanacaste's golden shores. A romantic Costa Rica couples adventure vacation blending fire, water, and sunset sails.

Couples

Secret Caribbean Hideaways

Escape to Manzanillo's quiet coves and Punta Uva's palm-fringed shores. The Caribbean side delivers intimate moments far from the crowds — a true Costa Rica couples adventure vacation gem.

Families

Sloth Trails & Calm Beaches

Gentle wildlife walks in Manuel Antonio where sloths and monkeys steal the show, followed by safe, shallow swimming at Playa Espadilla. An ideal 7 day Costa Rica family adventure trip highlight.

Families

Tortuguero Turtle Magic

Witness nesting sea turtles on the Caribbean coast's wildest beaches. Tortuguero's canals and wildlife create an unforgettable 7 day Costa Rica family adventure trip that kids will treasure forever.
